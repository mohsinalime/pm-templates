## Priority Levels

- 🔴 **High** — Must complete this sprint. Blocks other work or stakeholder demo.
- 🟡 **Medium** — Should complete. Important but not blocking.
- 🟢 **Low** — Nice to have. Move to next sprint if capacity runs out.
