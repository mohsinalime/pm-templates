## Risk Categories

- **Scope** — Requirements change, scope creep, unclear deliverables
- **Schedule** — Delays, dependencies, resource unavailability
- **Resource** — Team capacity, skill gaps, budget constraints
- **Technical** — Integration issues, tool failures, technical debt
- **Stakeholder** — Misaligned expectations, communication gaps, approvals
